Eligibility & Documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • Both parties must be of legal age: Male ≥21, Female ≥18 years.
  • No existing valid marriage (divorce decree or death certificate if previously married).
  • Mental capacity to consent & not within prohibited degrees of relationship (unless custom permits).
  • Special Marriage Act applicable to all religions & interfaith couples.

Required Documents

PAN / Aadhaar / Voter ID
Address Proof (Rent/Utility)
Date of Birth Proof (10th/Passport)
Passport-size photographs (6 each)
Affidavit (Marriage intention)
Marriage invitation (if applicable)

*Additional documents for divorced/widow cases as per guidelines
